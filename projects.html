TTA General Assembly

          Thinkathon is the sub-project of the assembly which gathers young people to discuss the cultural, economic, global problems and also the problems that youth of today is challenging and to talk the solutions that can be brought to them by the actions of young communities. Beyond that, the possible resolutions that today’s rule makers should  have for the discussed problems is being talked in the second step. The topic which is being discussed in this session are mostly related to the previous speech\ presentations. In the end of each session, the co-managers write down the highlights of the discussion and the resolutions which are found by the participants. The co-managers publish the notes that have been taken as the reports on the website ,which will be established soon, and also on the social platforms.
