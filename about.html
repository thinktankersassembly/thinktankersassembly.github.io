THIS IS THINKTANKERS' ASSEMBLY.

An assembly for Istanbul's thinktankers.

Improvidus, Aptop, quod Victum

Established in 2019, ThinkTankers'Assembly's main mission is to be a multicultural platform for thinktankers from distinct fields to assemble with other thinktankers to exchange and discuss their ideas, creating a generation that is not only having ideas, but able to express it, raise awareness and put them into realization.
